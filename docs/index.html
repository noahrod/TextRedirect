How it works

  1. Install the APK on your Android device.
  2. Sign in with your Google account inside the app.
  3. Grant notification access and contacts permission.
  4. Toggle SMS/RCS Forwarding on.
  5. Sit back while new messages are automatically forwarded.

The app never stores your messages on any thirdâ€‘party server.

All forwarding is performed directly from your phone using your Gmail account.
